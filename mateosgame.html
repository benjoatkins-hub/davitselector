Instructions:
Move = Arrow keys | Jump = Up | Attack = Z | Energy Blast = X (hold to charge).
Super Blast = R (uses Super stock) | Board Clear = T (uses 5 Super).
How to play: defeat enemies to raise your kill count, earn coins, and unlock God/Ultra mini games.
Retry mini games = Enter | Summon Jiren (when unlocked) = J | Spawn Jiren (once) = U.
Piccolo mini game (after Jiren): press P to start and unlock Piccolo.
Shop = S | Press number to buy/select avatars (1-5, 1000 points).
Shield weapon = 10000 points (buy in shop) | Use shield = K (30 second duration).
Switch forms with the buttons when unlocked | Fullscreen button is in the top right.
Reach 50 kills to unlock the first mini game | Win at 500 kills with all forms.
Kills to Mini Game: 100.
Don't use a board kill on a Mini Game.
On the win screen, press R + S to restart.
